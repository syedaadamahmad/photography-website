Qazi Hammad Mueenqari

Photographer

Qarigiri | کاریگری

noun: craftsmanship

Hi! I am a Kashmir based photographer aiming to push the boundaries of photography through unique perspectives and creative editing techniques. I specialise in urban, architectural and wildlife photography.

I have worked with many regional organizations to document flora and fauna across Kashmir. and have been actively sharing my passions and expertise online over the past couple of years. Qarigiri is an ever-growing collection of my work intended to be shared with an engaged audience, hoping to give glimpses of kashmir through my lens for everyone to cherish and treasure!

Feel free to contact me if you're interested in prints of my work.
